Mission: Impossible Characters

Ethan Hunt Luther Stickell Ilsa Faust
Ethan Hunt is the main character of the movie franchise "Mission: Impossible". He serves as an agent working for the Impossible Mission Force, abbreviated as the IMF.
He is assigned to a series of missions that he has to complete in order to save the world from danger, and in the process, does crazy stunts such as jumping off a ciff with a motorbike.
This shows his determination and physical prowess he needs to complete his missions for good.		 Luther is a supporting character who serves as the proficient and skilled hacker of Ethan's team.
With his hacking skills, he is able to get Ethan into places that seem unreachable.
This is why he can break into various buildings (even planes!) that have massive security systems. 		Ilsa Faust is a deuteragonist in the movies, and first appears in the 5th movie.
She is an assassin who worked for a criminal organization before helping out Ethan Hunt and his team to fight her former colleagues.
